About Predikt

A community-driven prediction market built for curiosity, not currency.

Our mission

Predikt exists to make forecasting fun and social. Every day, people form opinions about what will happen next — in politics, technology, sports, and the economy. We built a place where those opinions can be tested against fixed odds, tracked over time, and compared against a community of fellow predictors.

Unlike traditional betting platforms, Predikt uses no real money. Every prediction is scored in points, and accuracy — not bankroll — is what earns you a spot on the leaderboard.

How fixed odds work

Each market shows two outcomes — Yes and No — along with a multiplier for each. The multiplier reflects the community's current belief: the more popular an outcome, the lower its multiplier. Predicting a less likely outcome correctly earns you more points, because that's a better demonstration of forecasting skill.

Built as a learning project

Predikt began as a Web Application Development assignment at Strathmore University and grew into a fuller product exploration — combining front-end design, JavaScript interactivity, and structured version control practice.
