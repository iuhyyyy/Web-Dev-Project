Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about using Predikt.

Is real money involved?

No. Predikt uses points only. There is no deposit, withdrawal, or real-money wagering of any kind.

How are odds calculated?

Odds are fixed at the moment you place a prediction and reflect the current community split between Yes and No. The multiplier shown is what you'll earn per point if you're correct.

How do I earn points?

New users start with a fixed point balance. Correct predictions add points based on the market's odds at the time you predicted; incorrect predictions subtract your staked points.

Who decides how a market resolves?

Each market lists a resolution source on its detail page — typically an official announcement, news outlet, or public dataset — so outcomes are verifiable and not subject to opinion.

Can I suggest a new market?

Yes — use the contact page to send a market suggestion. Include a clear resolution date and source so it can be reviewed quickly.

Is Predikt available on mobile?

The site is fully responsive and works on any modern mobile browser. A dedicated app may follow in a future release.
